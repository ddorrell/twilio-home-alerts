Privacy Policy

This service is used only to send home automation alert text messages to phone numbers that I personally control or have permission to message.

The messages may include alerts such as freezer door warnings, water leak alerts, garage door alerts, security notifications, and other household automation notices.

Mobile phone numbers used for this service are not shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in information and consent are not shared with third parties.

Message frequency varies depending on home automation events. Message and data rates may apply.

To stop receiving messages, reply STOP. For help, reply HELP.
